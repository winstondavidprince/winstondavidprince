About Winston David Prince

Winston David Prince’s journey is a powerful testimony of unwavering faith, passionate worship, and a heart devoted to serving. His story in ministry began back in 2006, when he started volunteering in his local church. From early on, Winston was drawn to children’s ministry, Sunday School, and VBS — and it wasn’t long before he became an active presence in many church initiatives. With the encouragement of his church elders, he grew in every area — from media and worship to leadership and teaching — all while being deeply rooted in God’s Word.

From his school days, Winston displayed a gift for songwriting. While still in 9th standard, God began to inspire melodies and lyrics in him. In 2010, during his time at Karunya University, he contributed two original compositions to the gospel album Journey with Christ. That season birthed in him a deeper desire for music ministry. Later, in 2016, alongside his music brothers, he released Muttrilum Maarinen, followed by Christmas singles — each release reaching hearts and drawing people closer to Christ.

But behind the music lies a deeper story — one marked by both trials and God’s undeniable faithfulness.

In October 2016, Winston’s family faced a life-altering crisis. His father suffered a severe brain stroke that left the right side of his body completely paralyzed. Without hesitation, Winston left his job to be with his father in the ICU. It was one of the hardest seasons of his life — emotionally, physically, and financially.

Yet in the middle of the storm, God’s presence became more real than ever. He began speaking to Winston daily, especially through one powerful verse:

Job 5:9 – “He performs wonders that cannot be fathomed, miracles that cannot be counted.”

God didn’t just speak — He acted. Through the generosity of many and the love of the church community, God provided every need. The support Winston received — financially and spiritually — was a testimony in itself. The church stood strong with him and his family, becoming the hands and feet of Jesus in their time of great need.

Miraculously, God began healing Winston’s father. That season became a cornerstone of Winston’s life, shaping not only his faith but also his calling. Today, he’s releasing his first official music video — based on Job 5:9 — the very verse he sang in tears during those ICU days.

Now married to Hezia Golda and father to their beautiful daughter Deborah Princy, Winston continues to serve God with all his heart. Whether through music, ministry, or testimony, his life is a living declaration: God still performs wonders.
