About Winston David Prince

Winston David Prince is a worshipper, an evangelist, and a full-time IT professional — living every day on mission for Jesus. Whether he’s writing code or writing songs, his heart remains anchored in the presence of God. He believes worship isn’t just music — it’s a lifestyle. And every melody shared here is meant to point people back to the One who gives life. If you’ve ever felt unseen, unheard, or unworthy — may these songs remind you of who you are in Christ. You are chosen. Loved. Called. And never alone. Follow the journey. Worship boldly. Live fully. Subscribe and join me as we chase His heart through music.
